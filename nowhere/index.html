  • ABOUT WHERE HOUSE COMMUNITY DESIGN

Nowhere is an eyewear brand that started in 1992, and aims to create eyewear that
naturally blends with the wearer's personality. Recently, it has grown into a
more three-dimensional lifestyle brand by launching clothing and
miscellaneous goods, and provides the Nowhere experience
through its website and, Where house.
